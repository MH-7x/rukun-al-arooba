## Used Furniture Buying Guide for Al Ain Residents

New to buying pre-owned furniture? Here's what to know.

### What to Look for When Buying Used Furniture

**Check structural integrity:** Sit on chairs. Open drawers. Test everything. Wobbles, loose joints, or sagging indicate problems.

**Inspect for damage:** Look closely at surfaces. Minor scratches are fine. Deep gouges, water damage, or mold are red flags.

**Test moving parts:** Drawers should slide smoothly. Doors should close properly. Recliner mechanisms should work.

**Verify dimensions:** Measure before buying. That sofa might not fit through your door or in your room.

**Ask about warranty:** Always confirm coverage and return policies.

### Tips for Selling Your Furniture at the Best Price

Want top value when you sell? Follow these tips:

**Clean thoroughly:** Dust, vacuum, wipe down surfaces. Clean furniture photographs better and shows you maintained it.

**Make minor repairs:** Tighten loose screws. Touch up small scratches. These small efforts increase value.

**Take clear photos:** Good lighting, multiple angles, close-ups of any damage. Honest photos build trust.

**Provide accurate descriptions:** Note the brand, age, dimensions, and condition. Don't hide flaws.
